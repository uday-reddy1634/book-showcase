Key Lessons from
Rich Dad Poor Dad

1. The Importance of Financial Education

  • Schools focus on academic and professional skills, not financial literacy.
  • "The rich don't work for money; they make money work for them."

2. Assets vs. Liabilities

  • Rich people acquire assets that generate income.
  • Poor people accumulate liabilities that take money out of their pockets.

3. The Power of Entrepreneurship

  • Being an employee limits financial freedom.
  • Starting a business or investing can build wealth.

4. Understanding Taxes and Corporations

  • The rich use legal strategies to minimize taxes.
  • Owning a corporation provides financial advantages.

5. Mindset and Taking Action

  • Fear and self-doubt prevent people from becoming wealthy.
  • Learn from failures and take calculated risks.
