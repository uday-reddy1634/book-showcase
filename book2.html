Cashflow Quadrant - Key Points

A summary of Robert Kiyosaki's Rich Dad's Cashflow Quadrant.

Employee (E)

Works for a salary, trades time for money, seeks job security.

Self-Employed (S)

Owns a job, works independently, income stops if they stop working.

Business Owner (B)

Builds systems, hires employees, leverages others' time.

Investor (I)

Money works for them, earns passive income, focuses on financial freedom.
